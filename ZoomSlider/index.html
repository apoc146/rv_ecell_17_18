Glynk

You matter. Your tastes matter

Co-founded by Ramnath Shenoy in the year 2005, alumnus and best outgoing student of RV College of Engineering(Class of ’14). Glynk helps one build a network of people with whom one can share experiences, ask questions and recommendations–people who appreciate one’s choices, preferences and way of life. It is especially useful if you are new to a place, be it a fresher in college or a new employee at work. Users pick interests in which they typically make friends and answer a few fun questions. Based on their responses, Glynk automatically creates a like-minded network for them in real time. Glynk is a new, unconventional addition to the social networking space that enables a unique way of connecting and interacting with people.

Chronicle Scripts

Stressing on the importance of "detail"

Chronicle Scripts is an online repository of Detailed Knowledge. Co-Founded by a group of three enthusiastic sophomores-Anurag Bansal,Rohan Choraria and Suryaansh Sethia currently pursuing their degree from RVCE Bangalore,NMIMS Mumbai and SRM University Chennai respectively.
With a realization that most Start-Ups today, are running behind summarizing and shortening all content on the internet, there would be soon be a dearth of quality, and good research based write-ups for common readers to expand their knowledge upon. With currently over 10K monthly visitors, Chronicle Scripts aims to solve this very problem and delivers strong, powerful and enriching content.

Bums On The Saddle

Cycling makes you sexier

Bums On The Saddle (BOTS) is a Bangalore-based company retailing bicycles and cycling-related equipment and accessories. Founded by Rohan Kini, an alumnus of RV College of Engineering and a passionate cyclist himself, BOTS has established itself as a sustainable business, and is actively involved in the cycling scenario across the country. Operating as a physical-only retail space initially now provides their non-local customers an online store that would meet their cycling needs. BOTS set up a registry for cyclists across the country to register stolen bikes.

JUVENILE CARE

Be A Part Of The Change

Juvenile care, a city based charitable trust was established in 2010 by Rahul Prasad- proud alumni of R.V College of Engineering.He was inspired by an eye opening incident at New Delhi where in he came across lack of facilities for children from humble backgrounds. Today the organization has grown across five cities in India.It works by involving young Indians in social ventures and activities, and has over 15,000 volunteers working for child rights and child abuse eradication.The mission of the organisation is to give a platform to the youth to come forward and work for an alleviation of society.
