Events And Initiatives

" The secret of getting ahead is getting started "
-Agatha Christie

RV College Representative

An initiative meant to increase the reach of Entrepreneurship Cell, RVCE across the country, and imbibing a spirit of entrepreneurship within this generation of students everywhere.
RVCR will aim to reach colleges, which do not have the resources to maintain an Entrepreneurship Cell and hence will keep them connected to the current scenario of entrepreneurship through E-Cell, RVCE by appointing student representatives for a period of 1 year.

EXPEDIENS-B PLAN COMPETETION

Expediens is an intercollegiate competition centered around formulating a viable business plan for the hitherto unachieved realisation of an innovator’s idea. The competition is held in two levels: one for furthering the progress of budding startups, and the other for relatively inexperienced students aspiring to effectively kickstart their entrepreneurial careers

E-Summit

E-Summit is a dynamic platform that serves to inspire ideation and what entrepreneurial acumen one might possess via a plethora of competitive events, and speaker sessions given by an accomplished roster of speakers. The event serves to champion the rallying cry of modern economic times, namely, “Stand Up India, Start Up India”, which was the motto for thhe first edition of E-Summit. The second edition of E-Summit focused on the global nature of entrepreneurship spanning across themes such as FinTech, GreenTech, social entrepreneurship and techno-entrepreneurship.

EMBARK-IDEATION EVENT

Embark is an ideation and innovation workshop that comprises of a collection of activities to recondition creative thinking, business acumen and marketing capabilities. The event is aimed at providing hands-on experience, illuminating nuances in the viability of an innovative product and ultimately encouraging participants to pursue entrepreneurial aspirations.

EXCELSIOR

A one of a kind event, this bootcamp allows the vision of the budding entrepreneurs to grow into an enhanced version i.e. the participants will be able to finally actualize their idea into a physical, feasible model.Students from all over the country will be chosen on the basis of their business models and allowed an exclusive chance to participate in the free bootcamp. There will be a much-valued one-on-one interaction with experts from the various industries involving entrepreneurship and will also be given guidelines on how to pitch their model. The pitching will be of two types:
a) Elevator Pitch b) Detailed Pitch

EXPERIOR-INTERNSHIP FAIR

Experior is an internship fair to expose students to various summer internship opportunities. The fair saw a myriad of high-profile companies identify and recruit numerous outliers and innovators into their internship programs.

BANGALORE ENTREPRENEURSHIP CHALLENGE

The Bangalore Entrepreneurship Challenge is an intercollegiate championship involving the completion of a set of stipulated time-constrained entrepreneurial tasks. Teams of 4-6 work on the tasks assigned, bound by deadlines, with the release of each task only following the preceding deadline to inspire anticipation and renew enthusiasm among the participants.

LEADERS'S CONCLAVE

The Leader’s Conclave was an event which revealed to aspiring leaders through awe-inspiring stories, both heard and unheard, the key motivations and attributes that compose an accomplished leader brought to the students in first person. The speakers, charismatic leaders from diverse fields, sought to evince the essence of the belief of finding remedies rather than faults, so as to render the students’ aspirations actionable and inspire others to follow their example.

BUSINESS MARATHON

The Business Marathon is the premiere competition for budding entrepreneurs seeking to realize a multitude of innovative ideas, open to graduate and undergraduate students. Driven by a purpose of establishing a viable ecosystem conducive for ideation and the refinement of entrepreneurial skills, the event posits the achievement thereof through effective mentorship, recognition and connections to trigger an uptick of development in upcoming businesses.

TATA FIRST DOT

The Tata First Dot workshop, powered by NEN, was held in the RV College of Engineering. The event drew considerable footfall from various colleges, and among the key speakers were Mr. J.Ranganathan, Mr. Rajesh Kumar, and Mr. B.Deepak Kumar. The workshop’s main agenda was to introduce and inculcate business ideals in students and mentor them towards establishing robust ventures.This interactive workshop proved to be an immensely enriching experience for the 200-odd attendees, of which most had their own developing start-ups.

TEDX RV-Vidyaniketan

An event held under the umbrella of the pioneering TED organization, TEDx aims to ignite productive conversation and inspire connection in communities, organizations and individuals through screenings of videos of TED Talks, or a combination of live presenters and the aforementioned screenings. The theme for TEDxRVVidyaniketan was “Design for Change”; the event provided a common platform for various individuals to share their stories of innovative design inspiring radical change in the world. The event persuaded attendees to view design as a product of perseverance, imagination and the desire to surmount that which cannot be seemingly scaled..
